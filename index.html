hi my name is will

i'm currently a college student studying finance! this site is meant to be like a profile of sorts, showcasing my interests, creations, and more. click through the buttons on the right to learn more about me. maybe we'll have something in common 👀

the story of this site

this is the third rework of my site!!! it was originally just a portfolio that showcased my art, but now it does much more! i also wanted a space where i could express my love for the Y2K aesthetic and skeuomorphism. everything here is built or drawn by hand!
